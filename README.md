# üöÄ AutoAttend ‚Äì AI-Powered Automated Meeting Attendance System

AutoAttend is an **AI-driven system** that automates online meeting attendance by joining meetings on behalf of users, securely marking attendance, and recording sessions for later playback.  
It eliminates meeting fatigue, saves time, and ensures attendance compliance without requiring mandatory live presence.

This project was built as part of a **hackathon under Open Innovation**.

---

## üß© Problem Statement

Online meetings often require **mandatory live attendance**, leading to:

- Time wastage  
- Meeting fatigue  
- Missed sessions due to overlapping schedules  
- Reduced productivity  

Users are forced to attend meetings even when their presence is not actively required.

---

## üí° Solution Overview

**AutoAttend AI** solves this problem by:

- Automatically joining online meetings on behalf of users  
- Securely marking attendance  
- Recording the complete meeting  
- Allowing users to watch the meeting later at their convenience  

This enables **flexible participation** while ensuring **attendance compliance**.
